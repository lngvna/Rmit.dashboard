Collaborate Ultra

Collaborate Ultra is a virtual classroom and online meeting tool designed for education. It allows teachers and students to engage in real-time through video conferencing, chat, and interactive tools like polls, breakout rooms, and shared whiteboards. It's often used in schools and universities to deliver remote or hybrid classes and supports recording sessions for later viewing.

Features:

  • Real-time video conferencing
  • Breakout rooms for group work
  • Interactive whiteboard and polls
  • Session recording and playback
  • Chat and hand-raise functionality
